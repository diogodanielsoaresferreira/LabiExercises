Videos


Here are some videos that we found really interesting related to the website's topic.




"Dennis Ritchie Motion Graphic" by Rafaël De Jongh


Dennis Ritchie life resumed in a motion graphics video. More information on this topic here.




"Ken Thompson and Dennis Ritchie Explain UNIX (Bell Labs)" by VortexTech


On this video, Ken Thompson and Dennis Ritchie introduce Unix. More information on this topic here, or see the extended interview on the next video.




"UNIX: Making Computers Easier To Use -- AT&T Archives film from 1982, Bell Laboratories" by AT&T Tech Channel


On this video, Ken Thompson and Dennis Ritchie introduce Unix thoroughly through a film from Bell Labs. More information on this topic here, or see compiled interview on the previous video.




"The Computer Chronicles: UNIX (1985)" by Bob Blahloblaw


Here is another video about the UNIX operating system, taken from the Computer Chronicles television series. More information on this topic here, or see compiled interview on the previous video.




"The Code: Story of Linux documentary (MULTiSUB)" by Adam N.


Documentary about the creation of Linux and some basic concepts about Open Source Software. More information on this topic here, or see the documentary "Revolution OS" about Linux on the next video.




"Revolution OS - Documentário sobre Linux - Português" by Jardel Sacramento


Another documentary about the creation of Linux and the creation of GNU GPL license and Open Source software, subtitled in portuguese. More information on this topic here.




"Dennis Ritchie's video interview June 2011 by DennisRitchie.in" by Al Sahran


Dennis Ritchie Interview in 2011, few months before his death. More information on this topic here.



"Richard Stallman Free Software Song" by Ruddy Catalán


Song written and performed by Richard Stallman (founder of the Free Software Movement and of the GNU project), highlighting the advantages of the free software. More information on this topic here.




"Write in C" by Albert Veli


Comic song, pointing the advantages of C when compared to other languages. More information on this topic here.




"Free software, free society: Richard Stallman at TEDxGeneva 2014" by TEDx Talks


TEDx talk by the fouder of the Free Software movement, Richard Stallman. More information on this topic here.
