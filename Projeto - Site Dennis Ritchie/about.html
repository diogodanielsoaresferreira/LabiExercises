About



Who we are

We are two students of Computer and Telematics Engineering in the University of Aveiro. This website was created and developed as our first project for Computer Labs.



“Be a student as long as you still have something to learn, and this will mean all your life.”

Henry L. Doherty



Our goals

With this website, we try to display relevant information from Dennis Ritchie's life and show his importance for the modern computing. We also seek to demonstrate the content covered in Computer Labs classes. We hope that you can learn something with our work and enjoy what we have to offer.
